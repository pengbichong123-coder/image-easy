Workflow

3 steps
01

Pick an instrument

Choose a model, set resolution and aspect ratio, drop in reference images if you need them. The negative carries itself to the enlarger.

02

Compose the prompt

Describe the subject in plain language. We handle the camera settings, the seed, and the post-process.

03

Take the print

Ten to sixty seconds later, the image arrives. Download the high-resolution file, or expose again.
